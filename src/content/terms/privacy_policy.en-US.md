## Just Apps Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: January 1, 2026**
**Version: 1.0**

Just Apps ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA) of Korea, the Act on Promotion of Information and Communications Network Utilization and Information Protection, and other applicable laws.

### 1. Information We Collect

**a. Information Collected Through Google Sign-In**

| Category | Items Collected | Purpose |
|----------|----------------|---------|
| Required | Email address, name, profile picture (from Google account) | User identification and service provision |
| Automatically Collected | Service usage records, access logs, IP address, device information (OS, browser type) | Service improvement and statistical analysis |

**b. How We Collect Information**
- Automatically through Google OAuth 2.0 authentication
- Automatically generated and collected during service use

### 2. How We Use Your Information
1. **Account Management**: User identification, identity verification, prevention of unauthorized use
2. **Service Provision**: Providing app services and operating related features
3. **Service Improvement**: Analyzing usage statistics, improving user experience
4. **Customer Support**: Handling complaints, delivering notices

### 3. Retention and Disposal of Personal Information
1. Your personal information is **deleted immediately upon account deletion**.
2. Where retention is required by applicable law:
   - Records related to contracts or withdrawal of offers: **5 years** (Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce)
   - Records related to consumer complaints or dispute resolution: **3 years**
   - Records related to access logs: **3 months** (Protection of Communications Secrets Act)

### 4. Sharing of Personal Information with Third Parties
We do not share your personal information with third parties except:
1. When you have given prior consent
2. When required by law or requested by investigative authorities in accordance with legal procedures

### 5. Outsourcing of Personal Information Processing

| Service Provider | Outsourced Tasks |
|-----------------|-----------------|
| Google LLC | Social login (OAuth) authentication |
| Supabase Inc. | Data storage and authentication services |

### 6. Disposal Procedures and Methods
1. **Procedure**: Personal information is destroyed without delay when the retention period expires or the purpose of processing is achieved.
2. **Methods**:
   - Electronic files: Deleted using technical methods that prevent recovery
   - Other records: Shredded or incinerated

### 7. Your Rights and How to Exercise Them
1. You may exercise the following rights at any time:
   - Request to access your personal information
   - Request to correct or delete your personal information
   - Request to suspend processing of your personal information
   - Delete your account
2. You may exercise your rights through the Service settings or by emailing justkihyun1998@gmail.com.
3. We will act on your requests without delay.

### 8. Cookies and Automatic Data Collection
1. We may use cookies and similar technologies to improve the Service and provide a better user experience.
2. You may refuse to store cookies through your browser settings, though this may limit some Service functionality.
3. We may use analytics tools such as Google Analytics, and information collected through these tools is used solely for statistical purposes.

### 9. Security Measures
1. Encryption of personal information (TLS for transmission, AES-256 for storage)
2. Technical safeguards against hacking (firewalls, intrusion detection systems)
3. Restricted access to personal information (access control management, access log retention)
4. Minimization and training of personnel handling personal information

## Marketing Communications Consent

**Effective Date: January 1, 2026**

In accordance with Article 50 of the Act on Promotion of Information and Communications Network Utilization and Information Protection, Just Apps requests your consent to receive marketing communications.

### 1. Types of Communications
- New app releases and update announcements
- Events and promotional offers
- Useful tips and information related to our services

### 2. Communication Methods
- Email

### 3. Sender
- Just Apps (justkihyun1998@gmail.com)

### 4. Advertisement Labeling
All marketing emails will include the label "(광고)" (Advertisement) in the subject line, along with sender information and opt-out instructions, as required by Korean law.

### 5. Sending Hours
Marketing emails are sent only between 8:00 AM and 9:00 PM (KST). Separate consent is required for communications outside these hours.

### 6. Changing or Withdrawing Consent
- This consent is **optional**; declining will not limit your use of the Service.
- You may **withdraw consent at any time** through the Service settings or the unsubscribe link at the bottom of marketing emails.
- Even after withdrawing consent, essential service notifications (such as changes to Terms or security alerts) may still be sent.

### 7. Consent Records
The Company records and retains the date and time of consent, the content consented to, and the date and time of any withdrawal.
